## When You Need a Commercial License

A commercial license is required when you provide **client-facing portal access** — that is, when third-party end users (your clients) have direct login access to a Vibe MyBooks instance that you host or manage.

## When You Do NOT Need a Commercial License

The following uses are **free** under the Elastic License 2.0 and do not require a commercial license:

- **Individual use** — personal or business bookkeeping for yourself
- **Freelancer use** — tracking your own income and expenses
- **Accounting firm internal use** — your firm's own books, internal invoicing, internal operations
- **Staff-operated client work** — your accountants/bookkeepers use Vibe MyBooks to perform bookkeeping on behalf of clients, as long as the firm controls the instance and clients do not have direct login access
