**Q: Can I self-host this for my small business?**

## Accounting Firms

**Q: Can our accounting firm use this internally?**
A: Yes, free for your firm's own books and internal operations.

**Q: Can our staff use it to do bookkeeping for clients?**
A: Yes, as long as your staff control the instance and clients don't have direct login access. This is the standard "accountant manages the books" workflow.

**Q: Can I give my clients their own login to see their books?**
